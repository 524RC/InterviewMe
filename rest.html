About Us

Hi! We are three high school seniors who enjoy interviewing and hearing people's stories. Our objective is primarily to create more openness in society to allow people to share things they typically wouldn't with a stranger in hopes of breaking down social barriers that strangers subconsciously create toward one another. We aim to share their stories authentically, focusing on their passions, challenges, contributions, and enlightening experiences. Join us in our move toward a more interconnected society!



Introductions





Paolo

Hi! My name is Paolo and I’m one of the founders, directors, and personalities behind IntroduceMe. I am also the executive producer of the Teen Ventures magazine and president of my school’s Asian Heritage Club, Chess Club, and DECA chapter. Growing up, I have always been fascinated by stories of history and current events, this interest developing into a deep love of every facet of our world and its diverse peoples, cultures, countries, and politics. As a Global Studies student at Freehold Township HS, with this project, I hope to share my passion for international service and inspire greater community involvement in addressing modern issues.

Sawyer

Hello all! I'm Sawyer Chang, co-founder, editor, and interviewer at IntroduceMe. While I love the art of talking to strangers and discovering untold stories, I also volunteer my time in other capacities. These include captain of my school's Mock Trial and tennis team, president of our DECA chapter, and president of our Rhizome voter-advocacy club at Colts Neck High School's Law and Public Service Program. Through IntroduceMe, I aim to cultivate not only my speaking skills and amiableness but also widen my view of how everyone comes from a different background and has a unique story to tell.

James

Hey! I'm James Wang, co-founder and video director at IntroduceMe. My commitment to community building and empowerment extends to my involvement in school clubs such as the Asian Club, Chinese Club, DECA, FBLA, and LEO Club.
